How to Play

  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as they wish
  • Each result gets added to their CURRENT score
  • If the player rolls a 1, all their CURRENT score is lost and it's the next player's turn
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means their CURRENT score gets added to their TOTAL score
  • After holding, it's the next player's turn
  • The first player to reach 100 points on TOTAL score wins the game
